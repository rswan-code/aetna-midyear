Members activated. Barriers removed. Plans protected.

Across 8 markets, Pyx Health is reaching Aetna Medicaid members where traditional outreach stops — converting social and behavioral barriers into completed care. Every member enrolled is a relationship the plan now owns, with the data to prove it matters.

Talking Points — Opening Frame

  • This review covers 7 months of the Navigate program — from November 2025 through May 2026 — across all 8 markets.
  • The central question we're answering today: are the members Aetna most needs to reach actually being reached, and are they taking action?
  • The answer across the portfolio is yes — and the data we'll walk through connects what Pyx does to outcomes Aetna cares about: care access, quality performance, and cost risk reduction.
Program Snapshot 7 Months In — Navigate Program Performance

Talking Points — Program KPIs

  • 4,424 enrolled members means 4,424 individual care relationships established — not outreach attempts, not contacts. Enrolled members who have spoken with a Pyx care team member.
  • $802,760 billed to date reflects real program delivery. On an annualized basis, this program is on pace to exceed $1.2M in billable services across the portfolio.
  • 2.5 average months of engagement means the program isn't transactional — members are staying in. That sustained connection is what enables barrier identification and care activation.
  • 8,909 billable calls is a floor, not a ceiling — these are direct care team interactions, not automated touches.
