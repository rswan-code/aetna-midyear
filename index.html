The through-line
Aetna asked Pyx to reach members who weren’t accessing care. Across 8 markets, members are measurably healthier, lonelier members are less lonely, and members who weren’t in care now are. That is what this contract is producing.
The Population
The members who weren’t in care — and why.
Navigate was deployed to reach Medicaid members with the highest barriers to care: behavioral health diagnoses, tobacco dependency, maternal risk, and complex social need. These are members the standard care model consistently misses.
